Chained Cross-Page Continuation Experiment

Date: 2026-05-28

Purpose

This checkpoint tested v3_chained_cross_page_continuation as an experiment-only policy. The active policy remains v2_cross_page_continuation.

The experiment did not adopt a new policy, change canonical promotion rules, add OCR, add AI/model review, add retrieval, add embeddings, add graph work, or unlock downstream use.

Result

  1. Confirmed defect cp_000103 was fixed in the experimental output.
  2. Gold paragraph precision improved from 0.941 to 1.000.
  3. Gold paragraph recall improved from 0.941 to 1.000.
  4. Matched gold paragraphs improved from 16 to 17.
  5. Over-split paragraphs improved from 1 to 0.
  6. Over-merged paragraphs stayed at 0.
  7. Object-label accuracy stayed at 1.000.

Side Effects

  1. The experiment proposed 9 chained joins.
  2. 1 proposed chained join is covered by authoritative gold.
  3. 8 proposed chained joins are outside authoritative gold coverage.
  4. 57 chained candidates were rejected by the deterministic conditions.
The experiment is promising but not adoptable yet. The next gate is side-effect review of the proposed chained joins, especially the 8 joins outside authoritative gold coverage.
