Canonical Paragraph Review

Date: 2026-05-27

Purpose

Paragraph promotion created a downstream-ready layer, but promotion alone does not prove the promoted paragraphs are clean. This step reviews the promoted canonical paragraph layer before any retrieval, embeddings, reasoning, or graph work may depend on it.

This is a review layer, not a demotion layer. It flags likely risks and recommends whether the promoted layer is safe for downstream use under current rules.
