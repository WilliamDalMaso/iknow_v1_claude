Gold Review Framework

Date: 2026-05-27

Purpose

Two paragraph merge experiments showed the same structural problem: heuristic warning counts are useful symptoms, but they are not a verified answer key. This step pauses merge-policy changes and adds a small gold review framework for Douglass so future extraction changes can be judged against reviewed truth.

No extraction behavior changed. The active paragraph merge policy remains v1_consecutive_lines. No OCR, AI review, embeddings, retrieval, graph work, user-facing answers, or structure promotion was added.

Why Seed Rows Are Not Enough

The seed rows prove the workflow and artifact shape, but they are deliberately excluded from scoring. A row becomes policy truth only after visual inspection in the audit confirms the page image, bounding boxes, source lines, and candidate bucket. This prevents the gold set from becoming another layer of unreviewed assumptions.

New Rule

No paragraph merge policy may become active merely because heuristic warning counts improve. It must improve against authoritative gold review rows and must not worsen audit safety metrics.

Next Step

Use the audit to convert a small number of seed or new rows into authoritative gold rows. Only after that should another paragraph merge experiment be evaluated for adoption.
